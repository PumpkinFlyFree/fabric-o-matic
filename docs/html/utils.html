class FMstripes(*args, **kwargs)¶

Generatng periodic 1-dimentional stripes

Generates a signal indicating presence of stripes. The stripe is placed in the middle of period range.

Inputs:
  • t

    coordinate

  • period

    period

  • thickness

    amount of stripe in range

Outputs:
  • strobe

    boolean value, =1.0 where stripe is present

  • profile

    triangle value, =1.0 in the very middle, =0.0 at stripe edges
